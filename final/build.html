Microinteractions: Navigation bar and Search button

Triggers

  1. Hover on the headings on navigation bar
  2. Hover off the headings on navigation bar
  3. Hover on the Search icon
  4. Hover off the Search icon
  5. Click on the Search icon

Rules

  1. Hover on headings on navigation bar:
    • The text gets bolder and changes to a customized color (one of the 5 colors of Figma logo)
    • Two white border corners fly out from the text to the top left and bottom right corner (opacity: 0% to 100%)
  2. Hover off headings on navigation bar:
    • The text changes back to the original font weight and white color
    • Two white border corners fly in closer to the text and fades out (opacity: 100% to 0%)
  3. Hover on Search icon:
    • Cursor turns into pointer
    • The Search icon moves upward 16px then moves downward back to the original position
    • The animation is 1 second long
  4. Hover off Search icon: pointer turns back into cursor
  5. Click on Search icon for the 1st time:
    • A search bar (including a horizontal line and the "Search..." text on it) moves leftward from out of view into view, and fades in (opacity: 0% to 100%)
    • The search bar expands until it reaches the left border of the screen
    • A blinking cursor shows up on the bar, right before the "Search..." text
  6. Click on Search icon for the 2nd time:
    • The search bar moves rightward until it's out of view, and fades out (opacity: 100% to 0%)
    • The system records the entered search input (if any) and starts finding the matching results
