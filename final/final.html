Microinteractions: Search button and Heading "Topics" on navigation bar

Triggers

  1. Hover on the Search icon
  2. Hover off the Search icon
  3. Click on the Search icon
  4. Hover on the heading "Topics" on navigation bar
  5. Hover off the heading "Topics" on navigation bar

Rules

  1. Hover on Search icon:
    • Cursor turns into pointer
    • The Search icon moves upward 16px then moves downward back to the original position
    • The animation is 1 second long
  2. Hover off Search icon: pointer turns back into cursor
  3. Click on Search icon for the 1st time:
    • A search bar (including a horizontal line and the "Search..." text on it) moves leftward from out of view into view, and fades in (opacity: 0% to 100%)
    • The search bar expands until it reaches the left border of the screen
    • A blinking cursor shows up on the bar, right before the "Search..." text
  4. Click on Search icon for the 2nd time:
    • The search bar moves rightward until it's out of view, and fades out (opacity: 100% to 0%)
  5. Hover on heading "Topics":
    • Cursor turns into pointer
    • A straight line is drawn from left to right underneath the heading; its length is roughly the same as the length of the heading.
  6. Hover off heading "Topics":
    • Pointer turns back into cursor
    • The line is shortened until it disappears: the left end of the line moves rightward until it reaches the right end.
